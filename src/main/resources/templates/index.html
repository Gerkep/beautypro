ABOUT OUR CLINIC

BeautyPro is a pharmacist led, non-surgical medical Aesthetics Clinic which provides a range of treatments including anti – aging treatment, injected fillers, facial peels, dermal fillers, and a selection of cosmeceutical skincare.

We are committed to delivering a high quality and bespoke service to all patients from consultation right through to treatment and aftercare. Every treatment and product available to our clients, including prescription only treatments, has been chosen based on their safety, efficacy, and clinically proven results.

Our mission is to provide our clients with the best possible care to restore and rejuvenate their skin. We believe in offering natural looking results that will help our clients match their appearance with how they really feel on the inside.

OUR PRACTITIONER

All treatments are provided by Paulina Halagiera, our founder and director, who is a medically Qualified Practitioner (currently working towards Level 7 qualifications) and an Independent Prescribing Pharmacist.

Her passion for aesthetic medicine and constant desire to improve her knowledge of skincare ensures that she stays up to date in the dynamic skincare world and its latest technologies

Paulina takes pride in delivering excellent customer service whilst performing bespoke aesthetic treatments. Paulina is professional, approachable, and sure to make clients secure, confident and at ease in her hands.

“It is and honour for me to help my clients match their look with what they really feel on the inside, to improve their skin and the way they feel about themselves, to boost their confidence and with that they can better their opportunities in life.”
